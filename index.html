VISION

We are redefining how industrial data flows across machines, systems, and people.
Not by adding more layers, but by simplifying the whole.
The Unified Namespace (UNS) is not just an architecture.
It is a shared living understanding of reality, built collaboratively, evolving continuously.

VALUES

We value:

Reality from the shopfloor

over assumptions from the ivory tower

Small, immediate improvements

over large, theoretical transformations

Adaptation to change

over rigid execution of a fixed plan

Collaboration and feedback loops

over isolated decision-making

Adoption and change management

over deploying unused technology

PRINCIPLES

These principles are timeless, regardless of technology:

Report by Exception (RBE)

Scale by sending only what matters

Simplicity over complexity

Lightweight messages, fast understanding

Open over closed

No lock-in, full interoperability

Single Source of Truth (SSoT)

One shared, trusted reality in near real-time

Flexible semantics

Evolving structures over rigid models

Edge-driven intelligence

Decisions made as close as possible to reality

Resilience by design

Store & Forward over always-connected assumptions

Loose coupling

Systems connected, but independent

REALITY CHECK

There is no silver bullet | There is no “one-size-fits-all” UNS | Every implementation is context-driven:

→ legacy constraints

→ business priorities

→ risk tolerance

→ change management maturity

→ technical debt

→ internal skills & culture

Technology choices matter.
But understanding your reality matters more.
