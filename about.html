Your Data is Safe With Us

Under GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) guidelines, we assure you that your information will not be sold. Your data is displayed on our Open Source project for transparency and community building purposes only.

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal data. You can:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Modify your information if it's incorrect
  • Delete your data permanently
  • Export your data in a portable format

What Data We Collect

When you sign the manifesto, we collect the following optional information:

  • First, Last Name or pseudonym
  • Company Name
  • Optional message to the community
  • Country or Continent

Purpose of Data Collection

Your optional responses help the community understand which countries and companies are leading the way in adopting the Unified Namespace architecture for Industry 4.0.
